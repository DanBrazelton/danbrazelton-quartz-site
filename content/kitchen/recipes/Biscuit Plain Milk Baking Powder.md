# Biscuit Plain Milk Baking Powder

> **Quick Summary:** A simple and classic recipe for plain milk biscuits using baking powder for leavening.
> **Source:** Traditional

```cooklang
>> servings: 2
Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

In a large #mixing bowl{}, whisk together the @flour{350%g}, @baking powder{7%g}, and @salt{1%tsp}.

Cut the cold @butter{113%g} into the flour mixture using a #pastry cutter{} or your fingers until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

Pour in the @milk{200%g} and stir with a fork until just combined. Do not overmix.

Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and gently knead a few times until it comes together.

Pat the dough out to about 1/2-inch thickness and cut out biscuits using a #biscuit cutter{}.

Place the biscuits on an ungreased #baking sheet{}.

Bake for ~{12-15%minutes}, or until golden brown.
```
