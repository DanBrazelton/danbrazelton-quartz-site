# Salmon Cake
> **Quick Summary:** Simple and savory salmon patties are pan-fried until golden brown for a quick and easy meal.
> **Source:** Traditional

```cooklang
>> servings: 2
Drain the @canned pink salmon{1%can}.

In a large #mixing bowl{}, combine the drained salmon, @chopped onions{1/4%cup}, @yellow cornmeal{1/4%cup}, one @large egg{1}, @all-purpose or almond flour{1/4%cup}, @mayonnaise{3%tbsp}, and @chili powder{1/4%tsp}. Season with @salt{} and @pepper{} to taste.

Mix until just combined, then form the mixture into patties.

Heat @olive oil{½%tbsp} in a large #skillet{} over medium heat.

Carefully place the patties in the skillet and cook for ~{3-4%minutes} per side, until golden brown and cooked through.
```
