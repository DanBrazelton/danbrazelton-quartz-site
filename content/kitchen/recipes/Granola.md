# Granola

> **Quick Summary:** This is a classic baked granola featuring rolled oats, almonds, and coconut, sweetened with honey and finished with raisins.
> **Source:** Traditional

```cooklang
>> servings: 2
Preheat oven to 350°F.

In a #large bowl{}, mix the @rolled oats{4%cups}, @chopped or slivered almonds{1%cup}, @water{1/4%cup} and @melted butter{1/4%cup} together.

Then add the @honey{1/2%cup}. -- It mixes better once the oats are greased up.

Spread the mixture on a #cookie sheet{} and bake for ~{20%minutes}, stirring once at the 10 minute mark.

After 20 minutes, add the @unsweetened coconut{1%cup} and bake for ~{10%more minutes}. -- If you like a little darker, toastier granola go for 15 minutes.

Remove from oven and let cool completely. Stir in the @raisins{1%cup}.
```
