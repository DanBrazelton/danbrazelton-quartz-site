# [Bibb Salad with Black Truffle Vinaigrette + Mimolette](https://thedefineddish.com/bibb-lettuce-salad-with-black-truffle-vinaigrette-and-mimolette/)



![Bibb Salad with Black Truffle Vinaigrette + Mimolette](https://thedefineddish.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/Bibb-Lettuce-Salad-with-Mimolette-Cheese-4-scaled.jpg)

### Ingredients

- 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- 2 tbsp white wine vinegar
- 2 tsp black truffle oil
- 1/2 tsp dijon mustard
- 1  small shallot, loosely chopped
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp cracked black pepper
- 1 head bibb lettuce (also known as: boston or butter lettuce)
- 1/4 cup grated mimolette cheese

### Instructions

- In a small bowl, combine all of the vinaigrette ingredients. Whisk until well combined.
- Place the bibb lettuce in a large bowl. Right when ready to serve, toss with the vinaigrette until evenly coated. Top with mimolette cheese and a few extra cracks of black pepper. Serve immediately! Enjoy!
