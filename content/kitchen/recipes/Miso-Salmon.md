# Miso Salmon
> **Quick Summary:** Salmon fillets are coated in a savory-sweet miso glaze and broiled until perfectly flaky and caramelized.

> **Source:** Traditional

```cooklang
>> servings: 2
In a small #bowl{}, #whisk{} together the @light or yellow miso{2%tablespoons}, @soy sauce{2%tablespoons}, @rice vinegar{1%tablespoon}, @sugar{1%tablespoon}, @Sriracha hot sauce{¼%teaspoon}, and @kosher salt{¼%teaspoon} to create a marinade.

Place the @salmon fillets{1.5%pounds} on a #baking sheet{}. Season with freshly ground @black pepper{}.

Brush the miso marinade evenly over the salmon fillets.

Broil for ~{5%minutes}, or until the salmon is cooked through and the glaze is lightly caramelized.

Garnish with optional sliced @green onions{} and @sesame seeds{} before serving.
```
