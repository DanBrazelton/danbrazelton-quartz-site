# Pasta

> **Quick Summary:** A collection of simple pasta dough recipes using eggs, flour, and salt.
> **Source:** Traditional

```cooklang
>> servings: 2
>> This file contains several variations for fresh pasta dough. Choose one set of ingredients to proceed with the general instructions below.

-- Variation 1
@eggs{100%g}
@semolina flour{250%g}
@oil{1%tsp}
@salt{a pinch}

-- Variation 2
@eggs{217%g} -- about 5
@water{2%tbsp}
@oil{1%tbsp}
@semolina flour{560%g}
@salt{1/2%tsp}

-- Variation 3 (Hungry Boy Size)
@strong flour{270%g}
@salt{6%g}
@eggs{3%large}
@oil{7.5%g}

On a clean work surface or in a large #bowl, make a mound with your chosen flour and salt. Create a well in the center.

Crack the @eggs{} into the well. Add any @oil{} or @water{} specified in your chosen variation.

Using a #fork, gently whisk the wet ingredients in the center, gradually incorporating the flour from the sides of the well until a shaggy dough forms.

Use your hands to bring the dough together. #Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about ~{10%minutes}, until it is smooth and elastic.

Wrap the dough in #plastic wrap and let it rest at room temperature for at least ~{30%minutes}.

After resting, the dough is ready to be rolled out and cut into your desired pasta shape using a #pasta machine or #rolling pin.
```
