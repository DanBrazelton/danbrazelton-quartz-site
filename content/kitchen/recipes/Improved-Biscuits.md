# Improved Biscuits

> **Quick Summary:** These classic, flaky buttermilk biscuits are perfect for breakfast or as a side.
> **Source:** Traditional

```cooklang
>> servings: 8
Preheat oven to 425°F.

In a large #mixing bowl{}, whisk together @pastry flour{300%g}, @baking powder{12%g}, @baking soda{1%g}, and @salt{5%g}.

Cut in cold @unsalted butter{113%g} using a #pastry blender{} until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

Pour in cold @buttermilk{180%ml} and stir gently with a #wooden spoon{} until a soft dough forms.

Turn out onto a floured surface and knead gently 3-4 times. Pat out to 1-inch thickness.

Cut out biscuits using a #biscuit cutter{} and place on a #baking sheet{}.

Bake for ~timer{12%minutes} or until the tops are golden brown.

Brush warm biscuits with melted @butter{14%g}.
```
