# Pain de Mie

> **Quick Summary:** This recipe produces a classic, soft, and finely-textured French sandwich loaf using a levain starter.

> **Source:** Traditional

```cooklang
>> servings: 2
In a #mixing bowl{}, combine @White Flour{764%g}, @Whole Milk{184%g}, @water{305%g}, and @levain{149%g}. Mix until just combined and let it autolyse for ~{30%minutes}.

After the autolyse, incorporate the remaining @water{25%g}, @salt{15%g}, @Butter{100%g}, and @Honey{58%g}. Mix until the dough is smooth and strong.

Begin the bulk fermentation for a total of ~{4%hours}. Perform 3 folds during this time.

After bulk fermentation, let the dough have a bench rest for ~{35%minutes}.

Shape the dough, place it in a #loaf pan{}, and let it proof for ~{3%hours}.

Preheat the #oven{} to {425%°F}.

Bake for ~{15%minutes}.

Reduce the oven temperature to {350%°F} and continue baking for another ~{35%minutes}, or until the internal temperature reaches {205%°F}.
```
