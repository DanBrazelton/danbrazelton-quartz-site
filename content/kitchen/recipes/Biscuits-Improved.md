# Improved Leavened Biscuits

**Servings:** 8 | **Bake Temp:** 400°F | **Bake Time:** 12-15 mins

### 🛒 Ingredients
- 300g flour
- 10g baking powder
- 2g baking soda
- 6g salt
- 75g butter *(cold)*
- 170g yogurt or buttermilk

### 🍳 Equipment
- Large bowl
- Pastry cutter (or fingers)
- Fork
- Biscuit cutter or knife
- Baking sheet

### 📝 Instructions

1. **Preheat:** Preheat the oven to **400°F**.
2. **Mix Dry:** In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
3. **Cut in Butter:** Cut the cold butter into small cubes and add to the dry ingredients. Use a pastry cutter or your fingers to work the butter in until the mixture looks like coarse crumbs.
4. **Add Wet:** Make a well in the center and pour in the yogurt or buttermilk. Stir gently with a fork just until a shaggy dough forms.
5. **Fold & Flatten:** Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface. Gently fold it over itself 3-4 times to create flaky layers. Pat it into a circle about 1-inch thick.
6. **Cut:** Use a biscuit cutter or knife to cut the dough into pieces. 
7. **Bake:** Place on a baking sheet and bake for **12-15 minutes** until golden brown.
