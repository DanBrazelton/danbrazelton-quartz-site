# Radicchio Salad

> **Quick Summary:** A simple yet elegant salad balancing the bitterness of radicchio with the sweetness of raisins and the saltiness of olives.
> **Source:** Traditional

```cooklang
>> servings: 2
In a #large bowl{}, combine chopped @radicchio{1%head}, @golden raisins{1/4%cup}, and @castelvetrano olives{1/4%cup}.

In a #small bowl{}, whisk together @lemon juice{3%parts}, @boiled cider{1%part}, @salt{to taste}, @pepper{to taste}, and @sugar{to taste}.

Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine. Serve immediately.
```
