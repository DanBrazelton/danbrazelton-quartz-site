# Tandoori-Style Cauliflower


> **Quick Summary:** This Indian-style tandoori cauliflower is coated in a spiced yogurt paste with garlic, ginger, and garam masala, then roasted until tender and caramelized.

> **Source:** https://www.bonappetit.com/recipe/tandoori-style-roasted-indian-cauliflower

```cooklang
>> servings: 2
Place a #rack in the middle of the #oven and preheat to 400°F.

Finely grate the @garlic cloves{3} and the peeled @ginger{1%1" piece}. In a #large bowl{}, whisk together the grated garlic, ginger, @plain whole-milk Greek yogurt{⅓%cup}, @extra-virgin olive oil{¼%cup}, @Diamond Crystal kosher salt{1%tsp}, @garam masala{1%tsp}, @ground turmeric{½%tsp}, @Kashmiri chile powder{½%tsp}, and @freshly ground black pepper{¼%tsp} until smooth.

Core the @cauliflower{1%medium head} and cut into 1" florets. Add the florets to the bowl and toss to coat completely in the paste.

Arrange the cauliflower in a single layer on a #rimmed baking sheet{}. Roast until fragrant and starting to brown in spots, ~{20-25%minutes}.

Toss the cauliflower and continue to roast until very tender and browned, ~{15-20%minutes} longer.

For deeper browning, heat the #broiler{} and broil until the cauliflower is starting to char in spots, about ~{2%minutes}.

Let cool for at least ~{5%minutes}, then transfer to a #platter{}. Drizzle with @fresh lime juice{1%Tbsp} and sprinkle with @lime zest{1%tsp} before serving.
```
