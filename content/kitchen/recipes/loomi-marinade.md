# loomi marinade
> **Quick Summary:** A tangy and aromatic Middle Eastern marinade featuring dried lime (loomi), yogurt, and a blend of warm spices.

> **Source:** Traditional

```cooklang
>> servings: 2
In a #mixing bowl{}, combine @plain yogurt{1%cup}, @olive oil{2%tbsp}, @ground loomi{1%tbsp}, @paprika{1%tsp}, @ground cumin{1%tsp}, @salt{1%tsp}, @sugar{1/2%tsp}, and @cayenne pepper{1/4%tsp}.

Use a #whisk{} to mix all ingredients until the marinade is smooth and well combined.

Use immediately to marinate chicken, lamb, or vegetables for at least ~{30%minutes} before cooking.
```
