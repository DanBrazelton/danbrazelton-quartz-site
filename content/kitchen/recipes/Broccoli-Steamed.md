# Broccoli Steamed


> **Quick Summary:** This recipe outlines a simple method for steaming fresh broccoli florets until tender-crisp.
> **Source:** Traditional

```cooklang
>> servings: 2
Wash and trim one large head of @broccoli{}. Cut it into bite-sized florets.

Add about an inch of @water{} to a #pot{} and bring it to a boil.

Place the broccoli florets into a #steamer basket{} and set it over the boiling water.

Cover the pot and steam until the broccoli is tender-crisp, about ~{5%minutes}.

Carefully remove the steamer basket. Season the broccoli with @salt{} and @black pepper{} to taste before serving.
```
