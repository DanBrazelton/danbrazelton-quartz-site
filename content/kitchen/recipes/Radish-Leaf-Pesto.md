# Radish Leaf Pesto

> **Quick Summary:** This recipe transforms often-discarded radish leaves into a creamy, peppery pesto with nuts, cheese, and garlic.
> **Source:** Traditional

```cooklang
>> servings: 2
Toss @radish leaves{}, washed well, into a #blender{}.  
Add @nuts{}, @pecorino cheese{}, @garlic{}, and @olive oil{}.
```
