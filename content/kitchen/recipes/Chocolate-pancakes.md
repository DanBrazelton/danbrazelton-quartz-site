# Chocolate pancakes

> **Quick Summary:** A simple recipe for chocolate pancakes made by combining standard dry and wet ingredients.
> **Source:** Traditional

```cooklang
>> servings: 2
In a #bowl{}, mix the dry ingredients: @all-purpose flour{1/2%cups}, @baking powder{1/2%teaspoons}, a @salt{1%dash}, @sugar{1%tablespoon}, and @cocoa{2%tb}.

Add the @eggs{1} and @milk{3/4%cups} to the dry mixture. Stir until just combined; do not overmix.

Pour batter onto a preheated, greased #griddle{} or #frying pan{}. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
```
